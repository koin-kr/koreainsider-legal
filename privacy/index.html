Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-23 · Version: v1.1

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

The Company processes personal information for the following purposes:

  1. Account management, including sign-up and login (Kakao/Google/Email)
  2. Provision of Community/Curation services (uploading and displaying content such as posts, comments, and images)
  3. Receiving and handling reports, enforcing operational policies, and restricting use where necessary
  4. Responding to customer inquiries and handling disputes
  5. Ensuring service stability (security, preventing fraudulent use, incident response)
  6. (Optional) Providing marketing information (email/push) — only if the user provides separate optional consent

2. Data We Collect

The Company collects the minimum personal information necessary to provide the Service.

3. Children Under 14

The Company does not allow registration by children under the age of 14. If it is confirmed that personal information of a child under 14 has been collected, the Company will delete it without delay.

4. Retention and Processing Period

The Company processes and retains personal information within the period required by applicable laws or the retention period agreed to at the time of collection. In principle, personal information is destroyed without delay once the purpose of processing is achieved.

Exceptions include:

  • When retention is required under applicable laws
  • When a minimum retention period is necessary to resolve disputes or respond to customer inquiries

5. Sharing (Provision to Third Parties)

In principle, the Company does not provide users’ personal information to third parties. However, personal information may be provided when required by law or with the user’s separate consent.

6. Service Providers (Outsourcing)

To provide services smoothly, the Company may outsource certain personal information processing tasks as follows:

If analytics/error-tracking tools are introduced, the Company will update this Policy to specify the provider/items/purpose/retention period and notify users accordingly.

7. Overseas Transfer

The Company operates its servers in the Republic of Korea. However, depending on the operating policies of social login providers (Kakao/Google) and cloud service providers, some information may be processed outside of Korea.

If an overseas transfer occurs or transfer details (country/items) become specific, the Company will, in accordance with applicable laws, clearly notify users of the transfer country, items, purpose, retention period, and how to refuse the transfer, and will obtain separate consent if required.

8. Destruction of Personal Information

The Company destroys personal information without delay when the retention period expires or the purpose of processing is achieved.

  1. Procedure: Occurrence of destruction reason → internal review and approval → destruction carried out
  2. Method:
    • Electronic files: permanently deleted in a non-recoverable manner
    • Printed materials: shredded or incinerated

9. Your Rights and How to Exercise Them

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  1. Request access to personal information
  2. Request correction or deletion of personal information
  3. Request suspension of processing of personal information

These rights may be exercised through in-service settings or by contacting the Company using the contact information below. The Company will take action without delay in accordance with applicable laws.

10. Security Measures

The Company takes the following measures to ensure the security of personal information:

  • Managing access rights and applying the principle of least privilege
  • Encrypting personal information and sensitive data (where feasible)
  • Storing access records and preventing tampering/forgery
  • Security updates and vulnerability response
  • Establishing internal management plans and providing training (where feasible)
