What can the AI Camera actually do?
Four categories: Food (menu translation, vegan/halal/allergy info), Documents (labour contracts, visa forms), Life (Ttareungi bikes, T-money cards, signs), and General (auto-detect anything). Always verify safety-critical info independently.
How does multilingual translation work in the community?
Posts are automatically translated — write in your language, others read in theirs. No manual translation needed.
How is Kuration content personalised?
Our AI learns your preferences — food interests, travel style, destination — and surfaces the most relevant guides. The more you use KOIN, the better it gets.
How is my personal data handled?
Privacy-first by design. We collect only what's necessary, never sell your data, and comply strictly with Korean privacy regulations.
Which login methods are supported?
Kakao, Google, or Email — whichever is most convenient for you.
How can I contact or partner with KOIN?
For press, partnerships, or general enquiries, reach us at team@koreainsider.kr.
